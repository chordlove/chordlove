Chordlove

Chordlove is a tool that lets you write down chords and lyrics of your songs and share them with your friends.

Chordlove stores your work in the address (URL) of the page. To save your song, just bookmark/share it! To modify a song, make your changes and use the new address.

Basics

Use the back/forward buttons of the web browser to undo/redo changes.
